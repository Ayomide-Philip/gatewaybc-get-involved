Get Involved at Gateway Baptist Church

At Gateway Baptist Church, we believe that everyone has a unique role to play in our community. Getting involved is a great way to grow in your faith, build relationships, and make a positive impact. Whether you have a few hours a month or a few hours a week, there are many ways you can get involved.

Why Get Involved?

Getting involved in church activities and ministries helps you to:

  • Grow Spiritually: Engage in activities that deepen your faith and understanding of God's Word.

  • Build Community: Form meaningful relationships with fellow church members.

  • Serve Others: Use your gifts and talents to serve the church and the wider community.

  • Make a Difference: Contribute to the mission and vision of Gateway Baptist Church.

Opportunities to Serve

Children’s Ministry

Help nurture the faith of our youngest members. Opportunities include teaching Sunday School, assisting with Vacation Bible School, and helping with special events.

Teenager Ministry

Support our teens as they navigate their faith and life’s challenges. Opportunities include leading small groups, mentoring, and helping with youth events.

Worship Team

If you have musical talents, consider joining our worship team. We are always looking for vocalists, instrumentalists, and audio/visual technicians.

Hospitality Team

Welcome newcomers and regular attendees with a warm smile. Opportunities include greeting at services, preparing coffee and refreshments, and assisting with church events.

Outreach and Missions

Get involved in local and international outreach efforts. Opportunities include organizing community service projects, participating in mission trips, and supporting our partner organizations.

Prayer Ministry

Join our prayer team to support the church and community through prayer. Opportunities include participating in prayer meetings, leading prayer groups, and praying for specific needs.

Small Groups

Facilitate or join a small group to foster deeper connections and spiritual growth. Opportunities include hosting a group in your home, leading discussions, and coordinating activities.

Church Administration

Assist with the administrative needs of the church. Opportunities include office support, communications, and event planning.

Special Events and Programs

Community Service Projects

Join us in serving our local community through various projects such as food drives, neighborhood clean-ups, and outreach to those in need.

Annual Church Retreat

Participate in our annual church retreat, a time of renewal, fellowship, and spiritual growth.

Seasonal Celebrations

Help organize and participate in our seasonal events such as Christmas and Easter celebrations, which bring joy and the message of Christ to our community.

How to Get Involved

Getting involved is easy! Here are a few steps to get started:

  1. Pray and Reflect: Consider where God is calling you to serve and what gifts and talents you can offer.

  2. Explore Opportunities: Review the opportunities listed above and think about where you might fit best.

  3. Reach Out: Contact our Volunteer Coordinator, Lisa Roberts, at gateway4054@gmail.com or Call us to express your interest and learn

  4. Get Started: Once you’ve found your fit, jump in and start serving! You’ll be guided and supported as you begin your journey.
